**What-is-on-offer?**

→ Fully-funded PhD fellowships for talented researchers of any nationality at top-rated Portuguese centres.

→ Duration: Up to 4 years.

**Key-dates** (2026-call)

→ Application-deadline: 28 January 2026, 14:00 CET.

→ Who-can-apply?
- Researchers with no more than 4 years of full-time research experience at the deadline.
- Must hold a degree that allows PhD enrolment and must not have already started PhD studies.
- Must not have resided or carried out main activity in Spain/Portugal for more than 12 months in the past 3 years.
- Strong command of English (certificate required).

If you are interested, reach out to us through [email](interact.research.lab@gmail.com) with your CV and innovative research idea within the topics of our group.
